Black dyed hair at home with 5 natural ingredients are easy to find :

1. Dyeing hair with black tea

Special tea water color works to match your hair becomes darker and more shiny. People who suffer from premature gray hair or blonde hair color can use black tea to dye hair.

Perform

Step 1: You shot a specialty tea cups with hot water to cool down. The more specific note, the tea as black hair.
Step 2: Use cold water to wash tea so that the entire hair are wet hair by tea. Doing so about 10-15 times. The last time, made in about 15 minutes. Then, wash your hair with warm water until the wash water in, not the color of tea.
Note, remember to wear gloves when dyeing because tea stain hands if exposed to the skin longer.

2. Black hair thanks potato skins

An extremely simple, inexpensive help you blacken his silver hair but very safe, effective and not harmful to the scalp.

Perform

Step 1: Prepare the potato shells 250gr, 500ml water. Choose potatoes clean, chemical dip, should buy at supermarkets to ensure the safest offline.
Step 2: Shell potatoes were washed, a moderate amount of water, for all into the pot boil over about 20 minutes. Do not jump right off the kitchen, when boiling, reduce the heat and wait for 5 minutes to hair black agents in potato skins and then turn off the stove secreted.
Step 3: Wait for the mixture to cool completely. Use a brush to the cup, brushing hair from root to tip them. In particular, be repeated at multiple locations silver hair.
Within a few minutes, you can see the difference before and after fully implemented. Do not rinse but let dry naturally.

3. Rosemary shiny black hair

This is a very simple recipe, suitable for all women who have problems with hair color.

Perform

Step 1: Use 3 tablespoons dried rosemary leaves and boil about 1 liter of water 1.
Step 2: Wait until the water cools warm water, then apply to your hair and scalp massage. For about 5 minutes, then wash as usual.
Chances are, you'll need to repeat this step every time before shampooing but certainly this job extremely helpful. Rosemary leaves very benign, with the protective effect of natural black hair, feel free to soak up the first in the country offline.

4. Vinegar and soy sauce

This is pretty simple, does not use chemicals but still help your hair naturally black.

Perform

Step 1: You need to use half a cup of apple cider vinegar and add a half cup of soy milk.
Step 2: Use this mixture to massage after shampooing hair with shampoo, conditioner for replacement.
However, this usage is not encouraged with you allergic to the smell of cider vinegar or offline.

5. The great darkening from grapefruit peel

A secure method and more extreme natural that we would recommend is using grapefruit peel. This is a traditional method, making thick hair, strong, black seen.

Perform

Step 1: Use whole grain pomelo grapefruit peel and boil water to wash, can be combined with grilled locust. Diluted with cold water and shampoo to use.
Step 2: Gently massage on the head, wash it in about 5-10 minutes.
Another way is to use fresh grapefruit peel, squeeze the grapefruit essential oil directly onto the hair. With this approach, you must perform regularly in a certain time, will produce extremely great effect.
However it should be noted, with the natural way, you should be done regularly and repeatedly to have a dark hair color like the offline.
